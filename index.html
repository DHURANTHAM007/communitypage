About Us

Ashish Memorial ISC School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and challenging environment that encourages students to reach their full potential. We believe in a holistic approach to education, fostering academic excellence, creativity, and moral integrity. Our experienced faculty is committed to guiding students on their journey to becoming lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

Academics

Primary School

Our primary curriculum focuses on foundational skills in literacy and numeracy, fostering curiosity and a love for learning through interactive and play-based activities.

Middle School

In middle school, we encourage critical thinking and collaboration. Students explore a broad range of subjects to discover their interests and develop key academic skills.

High School

Our ISC program prepares students for higher education and future careers with a rigorous and comprehensive curriculum, expert faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities.
